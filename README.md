# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard — Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard that turns raw sales data into a clear, decision-ready view of business performance — covering overall KPIs, regional performance, category-level trends, and time-based sales patterns.

![Dashboard Overview]<a href ="https://github.com/Shaban-k7/Ecommerce-Sales-Dashboard---Power-BI/blob/main/Screenshot%202026-07-06%20003106.png">Dashboard Overview</a>

## 🎯 Project Objective

This dashboard helps e-commerce business stakeholders track sales and profit performance across states, product categories, and payment methods in a single view — making it easy to spot high-performing states, identify which product categories drive profit vs. losses, and understand customer payment preferences to guide inventory, marketing, and regional strategy decisions.

## 🔑 Key Features

- **KPI Summary Cards** — at-a-glance view of Total Sales, Profit, Quantity Sold, and Orders
- **Regional Performance View** — geographic/map-based breakdown of sales and profit by region
- **Category & Sub-Category Analysis** — bar/column visuals comparing performance across product categories
- **Trend Analysis** — time-series view of sales and profit over months/years

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- **Power BI Desktop** — data modeling, DAX measures, visualization
- **Power Query** — data cleaning and transformation
- **DAX** — - AOV (Average Order Value) = Total Sales / Total Number of Orders
- Sum of Amount, Sum of Profit, Sum of Quantity (core aggregation measures)
- Sample e-commerce dataset (youtube) — used for practice/portfolio purposes, not live business data.
